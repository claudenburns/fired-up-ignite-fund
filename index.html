Built around one question.

The fund was started by someone shaped by an exceptional coach and teacher — someone who believed, more than anything, that passion was the most reliable signal of a person's potential.

Not grades. Not connections. Not polish. Just the kind of commitment that shows up before anyone's watching and stays long after everyone else has gone home.

That belief is the foundation of this fund. We're not looking for perfect records. We're looking for the people who can't stop.
