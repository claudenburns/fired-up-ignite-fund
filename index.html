Founded in honor of a spark that never quit.

Steven Lindsay was the kind of teacher and coach who made people feel like their best days were still ahead of them. His energy was contagious. His belief in people was unconditional.

The Fired Up Ignite Fund exists to carry that forward — to find the young people who have that same relentless fire, and make sure they have the resources to keep burning.
